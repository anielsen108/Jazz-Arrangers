# Van Alexander (1915-2015)

Alexander Van Vliet Feldman, known as Van Alexander, was born in New York and led his own band before becoming a successful arranger. He arranged for Chico Marx, Les Brown, Gordon Jenkins, and others while scoring film and television. Alexander's arrangements featured sophisticated voicings with commercial appeal. His long career spanned swing era to modern arranging, adapting to changing styles while maintaining professional standards. Alexander's work demonstrated that skilled arrangers could sustain careers across multiple generations and musical styles.
