# Michael Abene (b. 1942)

Michael Abene is a New York-based pianist and arranger who became chief arranger for the WDR Big Band in Cologne, Germany. His arrangements combine sophisticated modern harmonies with strong swing feeling. Abene has arranged for countless artists and big bands, recognized for his craftsmanship and musical intelligence. His work with the WDR Big Band represents some of contemporary jazz's finest arranging, balancing tradition and innovation.
