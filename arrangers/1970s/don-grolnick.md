# Don Grolnick (1947-1996)

Don Grolnick was born in Brooklyn and became one of New York's most respected studio musicians and arrangers. He worked with James Taylor, Steely Dan, and countless jazz artists while arranging for various projects. Grolnick's style combined sophisticated jazz harmonies with rock and pop influences. His arrangements were tasteful and professional, serving the music perfectly. Albums like "The Complete Blue Note Recordings" showcase his versatile arranging across styles.
