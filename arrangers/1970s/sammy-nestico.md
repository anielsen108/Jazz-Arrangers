# Sammy Nestico (1924-2021)

Samuel Nestico was born in Pittsburgh and served as chief arranger for the U.S. Marine Band and later the Airmen of Note. He became Count Basie's primary arranger in the 1980s, writing charts that maintained the Kansas City tradition while sounding contemporary. Nestico's style featured clear, swinging arrangements with memorable melodies. His educational arrangements are performed by school and college bands worldwide. Albums like "Basie-Straight Ahead" showcase his gift for writing in the Basie idiom.
