# Tom Scott (b. 1948)

Tom Scott was born in California and became a leading fusion saxophonist and arranger. He led the L.A. Express and arranged for diverse artists from Joni Mitchell to Frank Sinatra. Scott's arrangements feature sophisticated jazz harmonies with contemporary grooves and production. His style successfully bridges jazz, pop, and R&B. Scott's work in film and television scoring demonstrates his versatility. His arranging represents peak Los Angeles studio sophistication, maintaining jazz credibility while achieving commercial success.
