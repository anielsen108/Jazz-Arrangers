# Vince Mendoza (b. 1961)

Vince Mendoza was born in Connecticut and studied at Ohio State and USC. He's won multiple Grammy Awards for arrangements combining jazz with orchestra. Mendoza has worked with artists from Joni Mitchell to the Metropole Orchestra. His style features sophisticated orchestration, contemporary harmonies, and cinematic scope. Albums like "Epiphany" demonstrate his gift for fusing jazz and classical idioms. Mendoza's work represents peak contemporary orchestral jazz arranging.
