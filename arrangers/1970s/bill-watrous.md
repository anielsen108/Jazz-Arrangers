# Bill Watrous (1939-2018)

William Russell Watrous III was born in Connecticut and became one of jazz's finest trombonists while leading his own big bands. His arrangements featured excellent brass writing showcasing his instrument. Watrous worked extensively in studios while maintaining jazz credentials. His arranging style combined technical excellence with musical substance. Watrous represented professional excellence in both playing and arranging, demonstrating that commercial studio work and artistic jazz weren't mutually exclusive when executed at the highest levels.
