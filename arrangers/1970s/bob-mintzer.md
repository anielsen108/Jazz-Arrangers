# Bob Mintzer (b. 1953)

Bob Mintzer, born in New Rochelle, NY, is a saxophonist, composer, and arranger who leads his own big band while being a member of the Yellowjackets. His arrangements combine bebop sophistication with contemporary grooves. Mintzer's style features complex yet accessible writing, with strong melodic content and exciting rhythmic energy. His big band albums and educational works influence contemporary jazz arranging significantly.
