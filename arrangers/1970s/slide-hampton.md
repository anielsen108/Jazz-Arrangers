# Slide Hampton (b. 1932)

Locksley Wellington Hampton was born in Pennsylvania into a musical family. He's a trombonist and arranger who worked with Maynard Ferguson, Dizzy Gillespie, and led his own bands. Hampton's arrangements feature sophisticated harmonies and excellent brass writing. He has worked internationally, particularly in Europe, and continues arranging into his 90s. His style balances bebop foundations with contemporary approaches, maintaining high musical standards across decades.
