# Bob James (b. 1939)

Born in Missouri, Bob James studied at University of Michigan. He arranged for Sarah Vaughan before becoming key figure in fusion and smooth jazz as pianist/arranger for CTI Records and later Tappan Zee/Columbia. James's arrangements helped define smooth jazz aesthetic. His style features lush keyboard textures, sophisticated harmonies, and accessible melodies. Albums like "One" (1974) and "Two" (1975) showcase his influential arranging approach combining jazz sophistication with pop sensibility.
