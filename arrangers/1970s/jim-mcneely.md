# Jim McNeely (b. 1949)

James Harry McNeely was born in Illinois and studied at University of Illinois. He's a pianist and arranger who has worked with the Thad Jones/Mel Lewis Orchestra (later Vanguard Jazz Orchestra), Stan Getz, and many others. McNeely's arrangements feature sophisticated harmonies, interesting textures, and strong compositional development. His work with the Vanguard Jazz Orchestra represents some of contemporary big band jazz's finest arranging.
