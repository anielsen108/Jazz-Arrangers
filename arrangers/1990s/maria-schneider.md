# Maria Schneider (b. 1960)

Maria Schneider's arranging reached full maturity in the 1990s with her own orchestra. Her cinematic arrangements tell stories through instrumental means. Albums like "Evanescence" and "Concert in the Garden" (Grammy-winning) established her as one of contemporary jazz's greatest arrangers. Schneider's style features Gil Evans influence combined with personal voice emphasizing nature, landscape, and narrative. Her work represents peak contemporary orchestral jazz.
