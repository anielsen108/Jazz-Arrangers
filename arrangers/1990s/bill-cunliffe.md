# Bill Cunliffe (b. 1956)

Bill Cunliffe is a pianist, composer, and arranger who has won Grammy Awards. He played with Buddy Rich and Frank Sinatra while leading his own groups. Cunliffe's arrangements feature sophisticated harmonies with strong melodic content. His style combines jazz tradition with contemporary approaches, demonstrating versatility across various contexts. Cunliffe represents professional excellence in contemporary jazz arranging, maintaining high standards throughout a diverse career.
