# Gary Smulyan (b. 1956)

Gary Smulyan is a baritone saxophonist and arranger who has worked with numerous big bands. He won multiple awards and leads his own projects. Smulyan's arrangements feature excellent saxophone section writing demonstrating his intimate understanding of the instrument. His style honors bebop traditions while incorporating contemporary elements. Smulyan represents the continuation of sophisticated mainstream jazz arranging, maintaining high artistic standards through dedication and consistent excellence.
