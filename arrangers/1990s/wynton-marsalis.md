# Wynton Marsalis (b. 1961)

Wynton Marsalis was born in New Orleans and became jazz's most visible ambassador as trumpeter, composer, and arranger. He founded Jazz at Lincoln Center and has led various ensembles. Marsalis's arrangements draw on entire jazz history from New Orleans to bebop. His style emphasizes traditional values and blues roots. The Lincoln Center Jazz Orchestra's repertoire showcases his arranging approach, honoring jazz history while creating new work.
