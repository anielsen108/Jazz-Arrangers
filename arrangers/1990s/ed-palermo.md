# Ed Palermo (b. 1954)

Ed Palermo is a saxophonist and arranger known for his big band arrangements of Frank Zappa's music. His work demonstrates how complex rock compositions can be arranged for jazz orchestra. Palermo's style features sophisticated voicings with rock energy. His Zappa arrangements introduce new audiences to big band jazz while honoring both Zappa's vision and jazz traditions. Palermo represents arrangers expanding jazz's boundaries through creative engagement with diverse musical sources.
