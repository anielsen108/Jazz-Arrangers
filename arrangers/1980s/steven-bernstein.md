# Steven Bernstein (b. 1961)

Steven Bernstein is a trumpeter and arranger who leads the Millennial Territory Orchestra and Sex Mob. His arrangements incorporate diverse American musics from vaudeville to funk. Bernstein's style is eclectic and irreverent, demonstrating wide musical knowledge. His work challenges conventional jazz arranging boundaries while honoring American music traditions. Bernstein represents downtown New York experimental jazz scene's arranging aesthetics.
