# John Beasley (b. 1960)

John Beasley was born in Louisiana and is a pianist, composer, and arranger. He has worked with Miles Davis, Freddie Hubbard, Steely Dan, and countless others. Beasley's arrangements feature contemporary harmonies and groove-based rhythms. He leads the MONK'estra big band, arranging Thelonious Monk's music for large ensemble. His style balances sophistication with accessibility, demonstrating Monk's music works in various arranging contexts.
