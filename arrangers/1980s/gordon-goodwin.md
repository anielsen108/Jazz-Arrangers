# Gordon Goodwin (b. 1954)

Gordon Goodwin leads the Big Phat Band, one of contemporary jazz's most successful big bands. His arrangements combine traditional big band values with contemporary production and grooves. Goodwin's style features exciting, high-energy charts with sophisticated harmonies. The Big Phat Band has won multiple Grammy Awards, demonstrating that big band jazz remains vital. Goodwin's arrangements appeal to both musicians and general audiences.
