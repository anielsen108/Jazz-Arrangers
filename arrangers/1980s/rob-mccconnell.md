# Rob McConnell (1935-2010)

Robert Murray Gordon McConnell was born in Canada and led the Boss Brass, one of North America's finest big bands. His arrangements featured excellent brass writing combining Canadian and American jazz traditions. McConnell's style was sophisticated yet accessible, maintaining high musical standards throughout his career. The Boss Brass won Grammy Awards and influenced Canadian jazz significantly. McConnell's work demonstrated that jazz excellence flourished outside major American centers, with Canada producing world-class arrangers and musicians.
