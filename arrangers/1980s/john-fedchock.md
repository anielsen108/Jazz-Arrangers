# John Fedchock (b. 1957)

John Fedchock is a trombonist and arranger who leads his own Grammy-nominated big band. He played with Woody Herman and Gerry Mulligan before forming his ensemble. Fedchock's arrangements feature excellent brass writing with contemporary harmonies. His style honors big band tradition while sounding fresh and current. Albums like "On the Edge" demonstrate his substantial arranging gifts. Fedchock represents contemporary big band arranging maintaining tradition through quality and innovation.
