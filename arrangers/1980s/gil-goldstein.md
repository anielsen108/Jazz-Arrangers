# Gil Goldstein (b. 1950)

Born in Boston, Gil Goldstein is a pianist, accordionist, and arranger who studied at Berklee. He has worked with diverse artists from Wayne Shorter to Michael Brecker. Goldstein's arrangements combine sophisticated jazz harmonies with world music influences, particularly Brazilian. His use of accordion adds unique colors. Albums like "Gil's Goldstein" showcase his harmonic sophistication and cultural versatility, influenced by Clare Fischer while maintaining personal identity.
