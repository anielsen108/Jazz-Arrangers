# Conrad Herwig (b. 1959)

Conrad Herwig is a trombonist and arranger known for his Latin jazz big band work. He has arranged Afro-Cuban versions of music by John Coltrane, Miles Davis, and others. Herwig's arrangements fuse bebop and post-bop with authentic Cuban rhythms. His style demonstrates sophisticated understanding of both jazz and Latin traditions. Albums like "The Latin Side of John Coltrane" showcase his unique approach. Herwig represents contemporary Afro-Cuban jazz arranging at its finest, honoring both traditions while creating personal synthesis.
