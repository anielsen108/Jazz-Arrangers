# Jeff Beal (b. 1963)

Jeff Beal is a trumpeter, composer, and arranger known for film and television work including "House of Cards." He emerged from jazz contexts, studying at Eastman, and maintains jazz credentials while scoring for screen. Beal's arrangements incorporate jazz sophistication into dramatic contexts. His style demonstrates how jazz arranging techniques enhance narrative storytelling. Beal's success represents the continued influence of jazz training on contemporary film and television music, showing how jazz skills translate across media.
