# Alan Broadbent (b. 1947)

New Zealand-born Alan Broadbent is a pianist and arranger who studied with Lennie Tristano. He has arranged for Natalie Cole, Diana Krall, and others, winning Grammy Awards. Broadbent's arrangements feature sophisticated harmonies and elegant orchestrations. His style combines jazz and classical influences, creating lush settings for vocalists. Broadbent's work represents sophisticated vocal jazz arranging at its finest, maintaining musical substance while supporting singers perfectly.
