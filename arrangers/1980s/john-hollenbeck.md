# John Hollenbeck (b. 1968)

Drummer and composer John Hollenbeck leads the Claudia Quintet and Large Ensemble. His arrangements feature sophisticated contemporary approaches combining jazz, classical, and world music. Hollenbeck's style emphasizes timbre, texture, and rhythmic complexity. His work represents contemporary creative music at its finest, demonstrating that jazz arranging continues evolving. Albums like "Eternal Interlude" showcase his remarkable compositional and arranging gifts.
