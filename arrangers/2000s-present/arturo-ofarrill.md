# Arturo O'Farrill (b. 1960)

Arturo O'Farrill, son of Chico O'Farrill, leads the Afro Latin Jazz Orchestra. His arrangements continue his father's legacy while creating his own voice. O'Farrill has won multiple Grammy Awards for sophisticated Afro-Cuban jazz arrangements. His work demonstrates that Latin jazz arranging remains vital, with each generation building on previous achievements while finding new approaches.
