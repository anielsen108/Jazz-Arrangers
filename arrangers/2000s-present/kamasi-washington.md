# Kamasi Washington (b. 1981)

Kamasi Washington is a saxophonist and composer from Los Angeles who leads large ensembles incorporating jazz, funk, soul, and hip-hop. His arrangements feature orchestral scope with strings, choir, and extended forms. Washington's style combines spiritual jazz traditions with contemporary production. Albums like "The Epic" demonstrate his ambitious vision. Washington represents contemporary jazz reaching new audiences through bold, uncompromising artistic statements that honor jazz history while embracing current sounds.
