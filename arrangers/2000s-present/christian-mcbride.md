# Christian McBride (b. 1972)

Christian McBride is a bassist and bandleader who has arranged for his various groups including his Big Band. His arrangements honor big band tradition while sounding contemporary. McBride's style combines bebop foundations with modern approaches. His work demonstrates that traditional big band jazz remains vital through quality execution and contemporary relevance. McBride represents mainstream jazz excellence, maintaining high artistic standards while connecting with diverse audiences through accessible yet substantial music.
