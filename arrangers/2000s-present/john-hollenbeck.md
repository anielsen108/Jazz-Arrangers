# John Hollenbeck (b. 1968)

John Hollenbeck's Claudia Quintet and Large Ensemble represent contemporary creative music at its finest. His arrangements feature sophisticated contemporary approaches exploring timbre, texture, and rhythm. Albums like "Eternal Interlude" and "Songs I Like a Lot" demonstrate his remarkable compositional gifts. Hollenbeck's work shows that jazz arranging continues evolving, incorporating diverse influences while maintaining improvisation's centrality.
