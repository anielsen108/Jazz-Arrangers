# John Hollenbeck (b. 1968)

## Biography - 2000s-Present Work
John Hollenbeck's Claudia Quintet and Large Ensemble represent contemporary creative music at its finest during the 2000s-present period. His arrangements feature sophisticated contemporary approaches exploring timbre, texture, and rhythm. Albums like "Eternal Interlude" (2009) and "All Can Work" (2018) demonstrate his remarkable compositional gifts, earning Grammy nominations. Hollenbeck's work shows that jazz arranging continues evolving, incorporating diverse influences while maintaining improvisation's centrality.
