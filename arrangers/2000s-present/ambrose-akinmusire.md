# Ambrose Akinmusire (b. 1982)

Ambrose Akinmusire is a trumpeter and composer who creates arrangements incorporating contemporary classical, hip-hop, and jazz. His work features through-composed structures with improvisation integrated organically. Akinmusire's arrangements address social issues while maintaining musical sophistication. Albums like "The Imagined Savior is Far Easier to Paint" demonstrate his unique voice. Akinmusire represents contemporary jazz engaging with current social realities while pushing musical boundaries through uncompromising artistic vision.
