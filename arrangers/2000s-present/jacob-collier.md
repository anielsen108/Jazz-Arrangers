# Jacob Collier (b. 1994)

British multi-instrumentalist Jacob Collier has won multiple Grammy Awards for innovative arrangements combining jazz, pop, and world music. His work features complex harmonies and digital production techniques. Collier's arrangements demonstrate how technology expands jazz possibilities while honoring traditions. His viral videos and educational content introduce young audiences to sophisticated musical concepts. Collier represents jazz's future, showing how traditional jazz skills apply to contemporary contexts creating music that connects across generations.
